InnoVision: Empower Your Ideas

A multi-phase program where students select a specialized "Innovation Pathway" and collaborate across disciplines to solve real-world challenges using the Design Thinking process.

Duration

14 Weeks

Framework

Design Thinking

Capstone

Internship Showcase

Program Overview: Choose Your Mission

In this program, you'll choose one of four Innovation Pathways. Each one represents a key part of solving real-world problems. You'll gain skills, complete design challenges, and collaborate across pathways to build a future-ready, sustainable community.

Phase 1: Passion-Driven Knowledge (Weeks 1-4)

The first phase focuses on deep, passion-driven knowledge exploration. Students choose a specialized learning pathway and engage in hands-on activities, research tasks, and reflective discussions to build foundational skills. This phase culminates in a "Passion Profile Presentation" where students summarize what they've learned and how it inspires their future innovation project.

Phase 2: Interdisciplinary Design & Innovation (Weeks 5-14)

Empathize

Deeply understand the needs and challenges of users within a chosen urban context.

Define

Clearly articulate the specific problem they aim to address.

Ideate

Generate a wide range of creative and interdisciplinary solutions.

Prototype

Develop tangible representations of their proposed smart city solutions.

Test

Gather feedback on their prototypes and iterate on their designs.
